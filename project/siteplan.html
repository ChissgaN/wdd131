Honda CB190R Repsol 2018 - Site Plan

Site Name

CB190R Repsol Fan Site

This name reflects my passion for the Honda CB190R Repsol 2018 motorcycle, focusing on its technical specifications, performance, and community knowledge sharing.

Optional domain: cb190r-repsol.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide useful information for current and future owners of the Honda CB190R Repsol 2018, including technical specs, common issues, upgrade suggestions, and maintenance tips.

Scenarios

  • What are the most common problems with the CB190R and how can I fix them?
  • Which upgrades or modifications can improve the bike’s performance?
  • What are the technical specifications and comparisons with other motorcycles?

Color Schema

The site will use colors inspired by the Repsol livery:

  • Orange (#FF4500) → accents, buttons, and highlights.
  • Dark Blue (#1C1B5E) → headings and navigation bar.
  • White (#FFFFFF) → background and text contrast.

Typography

  • Poppins (Google Font) → Headings.
  • Roboto (Google Font) → Body text.
